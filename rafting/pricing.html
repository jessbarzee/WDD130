Pricing

Bronze Pass

The Bronze Pass inculdes an 8 mile excursion, raft, oars, and an experienced river guide. Helmets, lifejackets and other gear can be rented from Dry Oar company. People are encouraged to bring their own gear, but gear will be inspected for quality before they are allowed to be used on an excursion.

Silver Pass

The Silver pass inculdes a 12 mile excursion, raft, oars, helmets, lifejackets, and an experenced river guide. People are encouraged to bring their own gear, but gear will be inspected for quality before they are allowed to be used on an excursion.

Gold Pass

The Gold pass include a 15 mile excursion raft, oars, saftey helmets, lifejackets, an experienced river guide, as well as a stop half way through the excursion where a meal will be provided. People are encouraged to bring their own gear, but gear will be inspected for quality before they are allowed to be used on an excursion.
