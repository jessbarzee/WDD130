Site name

The Rafting Co

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to advertise for The Rafting Co. and to inform potential customers about the rates and how to book of expeditions, the safety hazards and precautiosn, and the location of The Rafting Co. The site will also provide potential employees with the information they need if they were to apply to be a river guide. The site will also inform readers about the river they are rafting on and will be easily accessible to any who want to book an expedition.
