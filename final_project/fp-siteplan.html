Final Project Site Plan

Site Plan

Excessive waste is a big problem throughout the world. A big part of that waste is plastic, but another part that not many people are aware of is clothes. One of the biggest influences on clothing waste is fast fashion. Fast fashion is the term for cheaply made clothes that are produced quickly to consumers who then wear them once or twice and then throw them away. As if throwing away clothes was bad enough, the working conditions and wages of the people making the clothes is even worse. I want to build a clothing company that focuses on creating clothes that are long-lasting and environmentally friendly.

Target Audience

  • Where can I find clothes that are cruelty free and enviromentally friendly?
  • What is fast fashion and how can I avoid contributing to it?
  • Why is it important to shop "clean" fashion?
  • Where can I find durable long lasting clothes?
  • Where are the clothes from this site coming from?
  • What are the primary "ingrediants" or substances that make up the clothes om this site?
