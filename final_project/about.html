Why is fast fashion a problem?

If you were to ask someone what fast fahsion was, chances are they would probably have no idea what it is. Fast Fashion is an exponentially growwing issue that started about 20 years ago. The price of clothing became less expensive which encouraged people to by more clothes. However, the drop in price effected the qulaity of the clothes making them less durable. The poor quality of the clothes made it so people would throw out their clothes more often which added to the alreayd immense waste problems through out the world. Fast Fashion also has effects of the people making the clothes. In many underdeveloped countries, people are desperate to find work to provide for themesleves and their familes. Many find jobs at the clothing companies where they are forced to work in terrible conditions and only make around $10 a day.

How can you avoid fast fashion?

One of the best ways to avoid fast fasion, or to lessen it's reprecussions is to by used clothes. If that is not an option for you, or you would like to support small clean business, make sure you research into your clothing companies. Some clothing brands to avoid would be:

  • American Eagle
  • Shein
  • Romwe
  • H&M
  • Boohoo
  • Forever21
  • Zara
  • Victoria's Secret

Why is Phoenix Clothing Co a good option for me?

At Phoenix Clothing Co. we strive to use the cleanest materials. Organic Cotton is the main material in all of our clothing. By using organic materials, our clothes are hypo-allergenic for most people and also super comfy We also pay those who create our clothes a fair wage and give them free range to design clothes the way they see fit. By allowing our designers to deisgn their own clothes, we are encouraging their creativity while also supporting their businesses. We also allow our designers to incoporate thrifed clothes into their designs so that we are not producing as much waste as other clothing companies. Re-using clothes is one of the best ways to reduce waste and battle fast fashion. Just like the mythical phoenix is reborn after if dies, we belive that clothes can have that same oppurtunity; to be given a new life.
